Bubble Sort

Bubble sort is one algorithm used to sort a sequence of number. Bubble sort will compare two adjacent elements and swap them if they are in the wrong order.

Example:

Step 1 - Compare the first adjacent element which are 1 and 5.

Step 2 - 5 is greater than 1 so we will correct it by swapping their positions.

Step 3 - compare the next adjacent elements 5 and 6. 5 is lower than 6, they are already in the correct position we will not swap them.

Step 4 - compare elements 6 and 3. 6 is greater than 3. swap their position.

Step 5 - Compare 6 to 4. 6 is greater than 4. swap their position

Step 6 - repeat the process until everything is in their correct position.
